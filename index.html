Transfer anything

Instantly transfer a digital kilogram of gold anywhere in the world, securely and privately. GoatD creates the digital contract, signs it, and sends it for you. The Monetas notary checks to make sure everything is ok, then notarizes your contract.

Pricing

GoatD ships to run on a "testnet", so you can experience the system without worrying about real money or fees. In production, the end user pays 0.9% of the transfer amount, but that fee is capped at 0.30 CHF. Developers that build applications that run on the Monetas platform receive 0.5% of each transfer created by their application, and the remaining 0.4% goes to supporting and maintaining the network.
