Transfer anything

Send a digital kilogram of gold anywhere in the world with a simple JSON format request.

POST /transfers
    {
        "recipientname": {
            "goldgrams": 1000
        }
    }

There's no public knowledge of the transfer, and it's completely secure and legal, with a digital notary in Switzerland backing you up. How fast? Instant. The cost to your user? 0.9% of the transfer amount, limited to 0.30 CHF (about 0.30 USD). Application developers become Monetas business partners, and earn the majority of the transfer fees that are generated from the Monetas-powered software that they (you) create and distribute.

Need to exchange your Swiss Franc for Gold Grams? Or Bolivars for Bitcoin? It's just another simple transfer. Have a look at all the offers that are availble:
